Questions
Is Aiverture really free?+

Yes. Learning is 100% free, always. The tool directory is free to browse too — though some of the AI tools listed have their own paid plans, which we always label clearly.

Do I need any experience with AI?+

None at all. The learner library starts from "what is AI?" and builds up from there, at your own pace.

When does Aiverture launch?+

We're building it right now. Join the waitlist and you'll be the first to know the moment it's ready.

I'm already comfortable with AI. Is this still for me?+

Yes, that's the creator path. Skip the basics and go straight to browsing tools by what they cost and what they do.

Will more lessons and tools be added over time?+

Constantly. Both libraries are meant to grow as new AI tools launch and new topics become worth learning.
